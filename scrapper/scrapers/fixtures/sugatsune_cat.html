DOOR DAMPERS

Door Dampers absorb the impact when closing. By suppressing the momentum created by the closing door, dampers create a soft close effect. Sugatsune uses Lapcon technology to enhance door usability and convenience. Through viewing issues from the standpoint of a builder and architect, Sugatsune has created devices that are unobtrusive and easy to install. Select door dampers feature the ability to close at different speeds to better accomodate the user's preferences.
